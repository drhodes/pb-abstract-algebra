The goal of this homework is to review sets and functions. Some of the problems are qualitative and involve non-proof activities that should be done on paper or what have you. solutions will be provided.

If `A = \{a, b, c\}`, `B = \{1, 2, 3\}`, `C = \{ x \}` and `D = \varnothing`, list all of the elements in each of the following sets:

  • `A \cap B`
  • `B \cap C`
  • `A \cup B`
  • `A \cap (B \cup C)`
